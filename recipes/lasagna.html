Lasagna

Description

An easy and quick recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • 400g of ground meat
  • 400g jar of pasta sauce
  • A mixture of cheeeeeses
  • 1000 Lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 550 million degrees celcius
  2. Blacken mince in a frying pan
  3. Once the mince resembles charcoal, mix in the pasta sauce and simmer until it starts to burn onto the pan
  4. Chuck a handful of the extremely hot mince/pasta abomination into a medium sized baking dish
  5. Place just enough lasagne sheets on top of the sauce to cover
  6. Scatter 1-2kg of cheese on top of the lasagne sheets
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until all sauce has been used
  8. Place the lasagna into the oven and cook for 2-3 days, checking every 6-7 minutes
  9. Once the lasagna is cooked remove from the oven and allow to rest for 9 seconds
